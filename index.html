Nestled in Kerala, Aruvakode is more than a pottery village –
it is a living landscape where tradition, craftsmanship,
and everyday life merge seamlessly.

A living pottery village where tradition is shaped by earth and human hands. Experience Aruvakode — a place where heritage, craft, and community continue to thrive across generations.

Located in Kerala’s Malappuram district, Aruvakode is home to skilled artisans who transform simple clay into timeless pottery using age-old techniques. More than a production hub, the village reflects a unique harmony between everyday life and workspace, preserving a cultural legacy rooted in creativity and resilience.
