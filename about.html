Crafting Life, Culture,
and Community

Aruvakode is a living pottery village where tradition is shaped by hand and passed through generations. Rooted in community and craftsmanship, it celebrates the beauty of everyday creation while sustaining local livelihoods. Here, earth transforms into art, preserving culture and inspiring future makers.

Our values

Tradition and
Craftsmanship

At Aruvakode, pottery is more than a livelihood — it is a heritage shaped through generations. The village preserves time-honored techniques while nurturing creativity, ensuring that each handcrafted piece reflects culture, skill, and identity.

Community First

The strength of Aruvakode lies in its people. Built on shared knowledge and collaboration, the village supports artisans by fostering dignity in work, encouraging learning, and creating opportunities for sustainable growth.

Sustainable Living

Rooted in the earth it shapes, Aruvakode embraces eco-friendly practices that respect nature. By using local materials and traditional methods, the community promotes a balanced way of life that protects both environment and craft for the future.
