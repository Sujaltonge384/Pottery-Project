Clay Collection

Natural clay is sourced and cleaned to remove stones and impurities.

Clay Preparation

Water is added and the clay is kneaded until it becomes smooth and workable.

Shaping on the Wheel

Skilled artisans shape the clay on a spinning wheel, forming pots with precision.

Drying

The shaped pottery is left to air-dry, allowing moisture to slowly evaporate.

Firing

Dried pieces are fired in traditional kilns at high temperatures to harden them.

Finishing

Pots are polished or given simple surface treatments, ready for everyday use or display.
